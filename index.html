About Me

Hi there! I'm Jack. I am an 18-year-old accomplished scholar studying mathematics at undergraduate level at a university in London. I specialise in the fields of probability & randomness and statistics.

I've been a private tutor (paid and unpaid) on-and-off throughout my studies for the past three years, helping students at A-Level and below achieve the best grades they can.

Lessons

I offer a trial lesson to begin with, which is free. I request all students syllabus before beginning lessons, or their past academic work, to give me a better idea of what they need to learn. My lessons are offered at the same rate (£17 p/hr), whether it is in person or virtually. I have a document camera that allows the student to view what I'm doing on paper. I do not request the student to turn on their camera.

Prices

My lessons are reasonably priced at £17 an hour, whether its the evening, weekends or a bank holiday.
